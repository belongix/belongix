Still have questions? Contact us anytime.

Is Belongix really free?

Yes — the core platform is completely free forever. Jobs, salary insights, Career Score, Bexi AI, and 6 upskilling tracks are free. Pro (₹499/mo) and Premium (₹999/mo) unlock unlimited applications, mentor matching, AI resume review and more.

Is Belongix live right now?

Yes! Belongix is fully live at belongix.in. Sign up now and immediately access your dashboard, live job listings, salary insights, Bexi AI, and 48+ curated courses.

How does the mentor system work?

Browse verified mentors at belongix.in/mentors.html. Send a session request with your goal — mentors respond within 24–48 hours to confirm. Sessions are free or paid based on the mentor's preference.

What's coming next on Belongix?

AI resume review, salary negotiation coach, mock interview sessions and a mobile app are all being actively built. Pro and Premium members get priority access when they launch.
