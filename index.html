FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Who is Belongix built for?
+
Belongix is built for everyone at every career stage in India — college students seeking internships and placements, fresh graduates landing their first job, working professionals growing their careers, and career changers switching fields. If you have a career goal, Belongix is for you.
Is Belongix really free to use?
+
Yes! The Starter plan is completely free and includes job board, career assessment, community access, salary insights, resume builder, and learning tracks. Pro at ₹499/month unlocks advanced features including mentor sessions, AI job matching, full salary intelligence, and unlimited learning.
How does the Career Score work?
+
Your Career Score starts at 30 when you sign up and grows based on real actions — completing your profile, adding skills, connecting with professionals, completing learning tracks, and booking mentor sessions. It is a real reflection of your career readiness, not a fake number.
Are the job listings real and verified?
+
Yes. Belongix aggregates real job and internship listings from verified companies across India. We are also building direct partnerships with companies for exclusive Belongix-only opportunities. All listings are verified before being shown on the platform.
How does mentor booking work?
+
Pro and Premium members can browse verified mentors filtered by field, experience, company, and specialisation. You can book 30-minute or 60-minute video sessions directly. Mentors set their own rates and availability. Sessions are conducted securely through Belongix.
Is my data safe with Belongix?
+
Absolutely. All data is encrypted and secured using enterprise-grade security through Supabase. Your profile data, salary information, and career details are never shared with employers, recruiters, or third parties without your explicit permission.
When is the full platform launching?
+
Belongix is in early access / waitlist phase. The full platform with all features launches in 2026. Waitlist members get priority access, 3 months of Pro free at launch, and the ability to shape the product through our feedback program. Sign up now to secure your spot.
