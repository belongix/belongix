FAQ

Questions? Answered.

Who is Belongix built for?
+
Belongix is built for every career stage in India — college students seeking internships, fresh graduates landing their first job, working professionals growing their careers, and career changers switching fields. If you have a career goal, Belongix is for you.
Is Belongix really free to use?
+
Yes. The Starter plan is completely free and includes job board, career score, salary insights, Bexi AI career guide, and 6 upskilling tracks. Pro at ₹499/month and Premium at ₹999/month unlock advanced features like unlimited applications, mentor matching, and AI resume review.
How does the Career Score work?
+
Your Career Score starts at 30 when you sign up and grows based on real actions — completing your profile, adding skills, completing learning tracks, booking mentor sessions, and applying for jobs. It reflects your real career readiness, not a fake number.
Are the job listings real?
+
Yes. Belongix aggregates real job and internship listings from verified companies across India via the JSearch API. All listings link directly to the original job posting at the company or platform.
How does mentor booking work?
+
Browse the mentor directory at belongix.in/mentors.html. Send a session request to any mentor — they'll respond within 24-48 hours to confirm timing. Some mentors offer free sessions, others charge a rate they set themselves.
How are payments secured?
+
All payments are processed via Razorpay — India's most trusted payment gateway. We support UPI, credit/debit cards, net banking, and EMI. Belongix never stores your card details. You can cancel anytime with no lock-in.
