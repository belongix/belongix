The Story

Why we built Belongix

India's professional landscape is one of the most remarkable in the world. From engineering graduates to MBAs to creative professionals — India produces some of the most talented people anywhere. But they are largely left to navigate their careers alone.

Without salary benchmarks. Without structured mentorship. Without smart job matching. Without a community that truly understands the pressures and possibilities of building a career in India in 2026.

"Whether you're a student figuring out your first step, or a professional looking for your next big break — you shouldn't have to do it alone. Belongix exists to make sure no one in India navigates their career without the right tools, mentors, and community behind them."

We started with a simple website, a Gmail, and a big mission. We're building Belongix with the community — not just for them. Every feature is shaped by real feedback from real people across India.
