Quick Answers

Common questions

How quickly do you respond?

We respond to every message within 24 hours on business days. Email us directly at teambelongix@gmail.com for urgent matters.

How do I become a mentor?

If you have 6+ years of experience, fill the form and select "Become a Mentor". We'll review your profile within 48 hours.

Do you offer corporate plans?

Yes! We offer custom upskilling packages for teams. Select "Corporate / Team Plans" and we'll send you our enterprise details.

Are you open to partnerships?

Yes! We welcome partnerships with colleges, bootcamps, hiring platforms, and EdTech companies. Tell us your idea.

How do I report a bug?

Select "Bug Report" in the form and describe what you found. Screenshots are very helpful and we take every report seriously.

Where is Belongix based?

Belongix is based in India, building specifically for India's professional community. Our team operates remotely across Indian cities.
