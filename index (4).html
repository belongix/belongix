FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Who is Belongix built for?
+
Belongix is built for everyone at every career stage in India — college students seeking internships, fresh graduates landing their first job, working professionals growing their careers, and career changers switching fields.
Is Belongix really free to use?
+
Yes! The Starter plan is completely free and includes job board, career score, salary insights, Bexi AI (10 queries/day) and 5 learning tracks. Pro at ₹499/month unlocks mentor sessions, full salary data, unlimited Bexi AI and all 48+ courses.
How does the Career Score work?
+
Your Career Score starts at 30 and grows based on real actions — completing your profile (+30), verifying email (+10), adding skills, applying to jobs (+15), and completing courses. It's a real reflection of your career readiness, not a fake number.
Are the job listings real and verified?
+
Yes. Belongix aggregates real job and internship listings from verified companies across India. All listings are verified before being shown. We're building direct partnerships with companies for exclusive Belongix-only opportunities.
How does mentor booking work?
+
Pro and Premium members can browse verified mentors filtered by field, experience, company and specialisation. Book 30 or 60-minute sessions directly. Mentors set their own rates. Sessions are coordinated securely through Belongix.
Is my data safe with Belongix?
+
Absolutely. All data is encrypted using enterprise-grade security through Supabase. Your profile, salary info and career details are never shared with employers or third parties without your explicit permission.
