The Platform

What Belongix offers

🤖 Bexi — AI Career Guide

Our built-in AI agent answers questions about salaries, interview prep, career paths, skills to learn, and company culture — 24/7, free for every user.

💼 Live Job Board

Real-time job listings from LinkedIn, Indeed, Glassdoor and direct company portals — filtered and matched to your profile and skills.

💰 Salary Intelligence

Real market salary data for 15+ tech roles across Bangalore, Hyderabad, Mumbai, Pune and remote — know your worth before you negotiate.

📊 Career Score

A dynamic score that tracks your professional growth — based on your profile, skills, applications, and activity on the platform.

🎓 Mentor Network

Connect with verified professionals for 1-on-1 sessions — resume reviews, mock interviews, career switching advice, and salary negotiation coaching.

🛠️ Upskilling Hub

48+ handpicked courses from Google, AWS, Meta, Scaler, NPTEL, fast.ai, and more — with filters for free, certification, beginner, and advanced tracks.
